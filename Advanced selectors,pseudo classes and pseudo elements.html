Games and sports can be broadly categorized into physical sports, which involve physical exertion and skill, and mind sports, which emphasize strategic thinking and problem-solving.

Examples of physical sports include football, basketball, swimming, and tennis. Mind sports include chess, poker, and some video games. Many sports also incorporate elements of both physical and mental skill.
